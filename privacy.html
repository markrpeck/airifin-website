Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email address)
  • Financial data you connect through integrations (QuickBooks, Google Sheets, etc.)
  • Usage data and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process and analyze your financial data to generate reports and insights
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Financial Data

Your financial data is used exclusively to provide the services you have requested. We do not sell, rent, or share your financial data with third parties for their marketing purposes. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. Third-Party Integrations

Our service integrates with third-party platforms including QuickBooks, Google Workspace, and others. Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at mark@airifin.com.
