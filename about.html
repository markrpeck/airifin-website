The Founder

Meet the Founder

Mark Peck, CFA

Founder, AIrifin

CFA Charterholder Master Anaplanner 10+ Years FP&A

Mark has spent 10+ years leading Financial Planning & Analysis at Magic Leap, a venture-backed augmented reality technology company. He managed segment P&L reporting, led board and investor reporting, and built forecasting systems for the CFO and executive team.

He holds the CFA designation and a Bachelor's in Quantitative Finance from the University of Alabama.

AIrifin was born from a simple observation: the most valuable members of finance teams spend most of their time on work that AI should be doing. Mark built AIrifin to fix that — combining 10 years of FP&A experience with modern AI infrastructure so CFOs can spend their time on decisions, not spreadsheets.
