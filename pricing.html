Common questions

What's a "domain"?

A domain is a functional area of your business — Finance, Operations, Sales, HR. Each domain has its own AI agent tuned to that function. The Finance domain handles variance analysis, board decks, and cash monitoring. The Operations domain handles scheduling, vendor follow-ups, contract renewals, and inbox management. Most clients start with Finance and expand from there.

What happens after the 30-day pilot?

If you're happy — and we're confident you will be — we move to a monthly subscription starting on day 31. No lock-in after that. If the pilot doesn't deliver value, you walk away and owe nothing beyond the onboarding fee.

Do I need to manage the AI models?

Never. That's the point. AIRIFIN selects the right model for each task, manages API keys and rate limits, handles token costs, and upgrades your infrastructure as better models become available. You interact with your agent. We handle everything behind it.

What does "narrative learning" mean?

Every report your agent generates gets compiled into a financial knowledge base specific to your business. Over time, your agent learns your board's recurring questions, your CFO's writing style, your seasonal patterns, and your company's context. A March board deck in month 6 is meaningfully better than the one from month 1 — because the agent has 5 months of your company's history to draw from.

Can you match our existing report formats?

Yes — on Growth and above. We ingest your existing board decks, variance reports, and investor updates during onboarding, extract your format and structure, and configure your agent to match them exactly. Your team won't notice the difference except that it now takes minutes instead of hours.

How is my financial data protected?

Your data never touches a shared server. All credentials are stored encrypted in Google Cloud Secret Manager under your dedicated GCP project. On Enterprise, we deploy entirely within your own cloud environment. See our technology page for the full security architecture.
